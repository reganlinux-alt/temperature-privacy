Last updated: 2026-07-07

1. Information we collect and process

  • Temperature-related data: readings, battery level, detach state, measurement time, etc., used for display, alarms, and the history chart.
  • Bluetooth device info: the name and Bluetooth address of the connected patch, used for connection and auto-reconnect.

2. Information we do NOT collect

  • No personally identifiable information such as name, phone number, or ID documents.
  • No account registration or login.
  • No geographic location.
  • No third-party advertising or analytics SDKs.

3. Permission usage

  • Bluetooth (scan/connect): to discover and connect to the patch and transfer temperature data.
  • Location (only required below Android 12): older Android requires it for Bluetooth scanning; we do not read or use your location.
  • Notifications: for the foreground-service ongoing notification and temperature alarms.
  • Ignore battery optimization / auto-start: to keep monitoring and alarming while the screen is off or in the background.

  • All data is stored locally on your device. You can clear the history on the "History" page; uninstalling the app deletes all local data.
